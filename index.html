About Me

Born and raised in Taiwan, I moved to Canada when I was 15 years old. To overcome the language barrier, I forced myself to hang out with english speakers. I now hold dual citizenships from Canada and Taiwan.

Graduated from University of Toronto, I began my adventure in eastern Canada where I enjoyed the cold weather and ice skating in Toronto and Montreal.

But then I moved to Taipei and leveraged my engineering background to launch me into mobile payment career. Now, I'm a client side engineer who likes to dabble in new languages.
